Whipped Cream

This is a simple recipe for whipped cream. It's a base recipe that other flavors can be easily added to.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extarct
  • 1 tablespoon caster sugar

Instructions

  1. Add heavy cream to a food processor and blend until soft peaks form.
  2. Add the rest of your ingredients and blend until stiff peaks begin to form.
  3. Remove and serve, or store!
