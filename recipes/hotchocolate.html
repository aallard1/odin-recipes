Hot Chocolate

This is my very simple recipe for delicious hot chocolate. It is the perfect pick-me-up on a chilly day.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 8 oz milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Dry

  1. Add cocoa powder and sugar to a mug, stir to combine.

Wet

  1. Heat the milk in a small pot over medium-low heat.
  2. Remove from the heat when it begins to steam, try to avoid scalding the milk.

Put It All Together!

  1. Add a small amount of milk to the mug, stir the mixture to make a paste with ingredients. (This can help prevent lumping.)
  2. Add the rest of the milk and vanilla extract, stir to mix.
  3. Add whatever toppings you like!
