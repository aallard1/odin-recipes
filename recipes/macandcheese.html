Mac and Cheese

This is my recipe for delicious Mac and Cheese. You can use any cheese you like, though I prefer a half and half mix of young and aged gouda. My recipe is based off of one from this cookbook.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons all purpose baking flour
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (whatever cheese you like, though I'd avoid mozzarella as it doesn't blend well)
  • salt and pepper to taste (the world is your oyster! add whatever spices you like!)
  • 1/2 box macaroni noodles

Instructions

Rue

  1. Add butter to small pot, melt over low heat.
  2. Add flour and stir to combine. Turn up heat to medium-low and stir for 2-4 minutes or until mixture smells slightly nutty.
  3. Add milk slowly, then reduce heat to low. Stir occasionally until rue has thickened slightly.
  4. Add cheese and stir to combine. Heat may be increased to aid in melting the cheese.
  5. Season to taste, then set aside. Stir occasionally to keep sauce homogeneous.

Pasta

  1. Boil slightly salted water in a large pot.
  2. When ready, add pasta. Cook as directed.
  3. Drain pasta in colender. Then, rinse with cold water to prevent further cooking.

Put It All Together!

  1. Add pasta to sauce. Stir over medium-low heat.
  2. It's ready to eat!
  3. If desired, bake in oven safe casserole dish for 15 minutes at 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
